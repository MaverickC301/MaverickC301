Davao City

Davao del Norte is primarily an agricultural province. No wonder it is hailed as the Banana Capital of the Philippines, with many plantations run by multinationals and local producers. However, the province also boasts of its vast array of tourist attractions. To all beach bums and those who love adventure, explore the great diving sites and fine beaches of Island Garden City of Samal. With the fast infrastructure development in its capital city, Tagum, MICE and sports tourism is also becoming popular in the province.

Zamboanga City

Zamboanga City, city and port, western Mindanao, Philippines. It is a busy port strategically located on the southwestern tip of the Zamboanga Peninsula, on Basilan Strait and sheltered by Basilan island. The immediate coastal lowlands are narrow, with low, rugged hills located a short distance inland.
